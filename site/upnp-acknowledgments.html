Acknowledgments

The Felix UPnP base driver and related bundles were originally developed by the Domoware project which targeted the OSGi R3. The driver is based on a modified version of the "UPnP for Java" library released by the Cyberlink project. This version, called CyberDomo, is currently maintained by the Domoware project team and aligned to the Cyberlink library regularly.
